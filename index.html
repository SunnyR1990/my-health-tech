About Our Founder

Founded by a medical professional with extensive experience from 2016 to 2025, HealthTech.co was born out of a desire to bridge the gap between clinical practice and cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to create solutions that are not just innovative, but also deeply rooted in the realities of patient care. We believe that technology should empower, not complicate, and our platform is built on this core principle.

Our journey began with firsthand experience in the healthcare system, understanding its challenges and opportunities. We've brought together a team of experts in medicine, engineering, and data science to build a company that truly understands the needs of both patients and providers.
